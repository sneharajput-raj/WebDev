Tourist Places in Madhya Pradesh

1. Bhopal

Bhopal is the capital of Madhya Pradesh. It is known as the lake city. There are many tourist places like upper and lower lake, hanuman tekri, van vihar, bhojpur and many more.

2. Indore

Indore is the cleanest city of India. It is famous for delicious food Poha Jalebi. Many famous celebraties like Lata Mangeshkar, Salman khan, Rajat Patidar are from here. Tourist places are 56 dukan, Khajrana Ganesh mandir, Ranjit hanuman, Pitraparvat, Rajwada, Jam gate etc.

3. Ujjain

Ujjain is famous for one of the 12 jyoterlingas named Mahakaleshwar jyoterlinga, where early morning bhasm aarti is famous. Kal Bharav is also a pilgrimage place.

4. Narmadapuram

It is formarly known as Hoshangabad built by Husang Shah Gori in 1405-1406 AD. It is situated on bank of Narmada River. There is famous Kale Mahadev temple on bank of Sethani Ghat. Pachmari hill station is here. Itarsi junction is the largest railway station of MP.

5. Khandwa

It is famous for Omkareshwar jyoterlinga which is surrounded by Maa Narmada and is in the shape of "OM".
